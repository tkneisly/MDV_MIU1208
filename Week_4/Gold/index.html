Movie Night

Want to go to the movies?

Was there ever a movie you wanted to go see, in the spur of the moment, but none of your friends were able to go with you?

Or maybe there was a big movie event, like a premier, and you wanted to go even though you had no one to go with?

"Movie Night" is a social-search app that helps connect people who want to go to the movies, right now! Just set the types of friends you prefer, and your taste in movies, and the app will find make the connection for you!

How does the app work?

The app works two ways:

First, it can work from a saved search. Just set your preferences, and the app will find other registered users in your preferred area who are also looking to see similar movies with similar groups of people. When the app makes a match, all the matched users get an alert with the movie, time, location, and a unique Twitter hash tag.

Second, by browsing movies from the home screen, you can pick a movie you want to see. The movie you want to see can be a movie in theaters now, or a future release. 30 minutes before the movie starts, Movie Night will let you know how many other Movie Night users are at your chosen movie, and will provide you and those users with a unique Twitter hash tag

What's with the Twitter hash tag?

The Twitter hash tag is a way for users to organize with each other after they're matched together for a movie.

Once a match is made, users can use the Twitter tag to pick a meeting place, or figure out how to get great seats. Or whatever!

Keep in mind! Tweets are public, so don't share any reallly personal information. Also, always be cautious when meeting new people!

Just sayin'!
